# 🧠 App Brainstorming – Group Project


# 💡 Initial Brainstorming Ideas (6 Ideas Minimum)

## 1. Howard Sync (All-in-One Campus App)
**Category:** Education / Productivity / Social  

An all-in-one platform designed specifically for Howard University students to simplify and enhance campus life.

**Key Features:**
- Class schedules + assignment tracking  
- Campus navigation with building maps  
- Event discovery and organization feeds  
- Safety features (alerts, safe routes)  
- Chat system for students and organizations  
- Dining menus and wait times  

---

## 2. StudySprint (Gamified Studying)
**Category:** Education / Productivity  

A productivity app that turns studying into a game.

**Key Features:**
- Pomodoro timer with streaks  
- Leaderboards among friends  
- Study goals and rewards  
- Focus analytics  

---

## 3. TripLink (Social Travel Planner)
**Category:** Travel / Social  

A collaborative trip planning app for groups.

**Key Features:**
- Shared itineraries  
- Voting system for decisions  
- Budget splitting  
- Interactive maps  

---

## 4. FitSnap AI (AI Fitness Coach)
**Category:** Health & Fitness  

An AI-powered app for tracking fitness and nutrition.

**Key Features:**
- Meal scanning for calories/macros  
- Workout tracking  
- Form correction using camera  
- Personalized plans  

---

## 5. MoodStream (Emotion-Based Entertainment)
**Category:** Entertainment  

An app that recommends content based on user mood.

**Key Features:**
- Mood-based suggestions  
- Music and movie recommendations  
- Mood tracking analytics  

---

## 6. SkillSwap (Peer-to-Peer Learning)
**Category:** Social / Education  

A platform where users exchange skills with each other.

**Key Features:**
- Skill matching system  
- Scheduling sessions  
- Ratings and feedback  
- Community-driven learning

# 🏆 Evaluation of Top 3 Ideas

- Strong potential for adoption  
- Scalable with AI features  

**Cons:**
- Complex to build (many features)  
- Requires integration with multiple systems

- Innovative use of AI

# ✅ Final App Idea Chosen

## 🎯 Howard Sync

We selected **Howard Sync** as our final app idea because:

- It directly addresses real student needs at Howard University  
- Combines multiple useful features into one platform  
- Has strong impact potential on student life  
- Allows room for innovation (AI chatbot, smart navigation, etc.)

**Vision:**  
To create a centralized digital ecosystem that enhances student life, improves accessibility to campus resources, and fosters stronger community engagement.
